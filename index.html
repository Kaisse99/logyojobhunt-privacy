Your Privacy Matters

Log Yo Job Hunt is built on a simple principle: your job search data is yours alone. This policy explains exactly how we handle — or more accurately, don't handle — your information.

📱

Local Data Storage

All your job application data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or have iCloud sync enabled.

✓  No servers. No databases. No third-party storage. Your data lives on your device.

🚫

Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal information. This includes:

  • Job application details and company information
  • Contact information or interview notes
  • Attachments or documents you add
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Device identifiers or crash reports sent to us

Your data belongs to you, period.

☁️

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your data syncs across your Apple devices through Apple's CloudKit service. This sync is:

  • Controlled entirely by your iCloud settings
  • Encrypted end-to-end by Apple
  • Not accessible to us in any form

Apple's own privacy policy governs iCloud data. You can manage or disable iCloud sync anytime in your device Settings. Apple Privacy Policy →

🔔

Local Notifications

When you enable notifications for follow-up reminders or interview dates, all reminders are generated and delivered entirely on your device. No notification content or scheduling data is sent to external servers.

📤

Data Export & Control

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Export to CSV anytime from within the app
  • All exports are stored locally on your device
  • Delete all data permanently with one tap
  • No backups are stored on our servers
🌐

Third-Party Services

Log Yo Job Hunt does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app subscriptions — Apple's privacy policy governs that interaction. Beyond that, the app has no external dependencies.

✅

Your Rights

At any time, you can:

  • Access all your data directly within the app
  • Export your data in standard CSV format
  • Delete your data permanently from Settings
  • Use the app without creating an account
  • Disable notifications at any time
  • Disable iCloud sync through your device settings
👶

Children's Privacy

Log Yo Job Hunt is intended for job seekers aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under the age of 16. Since we collect no user data whatsoever, younger users face no data privacy risk from using the app — however, the app's content is intended for adults entering the workforce.

📄

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. We'll always communicate material changes through app update notes. If you continue using the app after an update, we take that as your acknowledgment of the revised policy.
